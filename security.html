Services


We offer you a total solution for your security needs. After a comprehensive risk analysis, we create the security concept for the people at risk. In doing so, we not only consider the "security risks" of the protected persons, but also evaluate the everyday dangers such as accidents and illness at home and abroad.


Upon request, we can take over the implementation of the proposed security measures. We guarantee the safety of our customers at all times by clearing up safe routes, rooms and objects, by armed object and escort protection and by special driving services.


Good coordination with the state security organs is essential when there is a high risk. We have a large network of authorities, the military and the police and are happy to bring this to bear for our customers.
